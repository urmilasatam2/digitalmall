The company provides customizable reports to the care team and provides a summary of adherence and insights gleaned from daily video submissions. It offers miDOT, an application that improves medication adherence and links patients to care; and outbreak monitoring systems for public health officials that enable them to conduct active and direct-active monitoring of people.
